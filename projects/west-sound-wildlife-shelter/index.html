Challenge

With an outdated design on their online presence, the organization needs a visual refresh that matches their current identity and mission. 

Background

The West Sound Wildlife Shelter is a non-profit organization based in Washington that provides “injured, orphaned, & sick wildlife a second chance at wildlife and promotes the well-being of wildlife & their habitats through public outreach, education, and involvement”.

The Design Process

The first step in redesigning and creating a visual refresh was doing a brand audit of the organization. With a team of five others, we explored their website and noted several instances of successful design as well as unsuccessful design (pain points).
